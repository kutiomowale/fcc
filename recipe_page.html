Beans Recipe

This is a great beans recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of beans
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 1 cube of seasoning
  • 1 tea spoon of salt
  • 2 tea spoon of grounded pepper
  • 1 spoon of palm oil

Instructions

  1. Wash the beans, slice and add the onions. Add the seasoning . Boil the beans until it is soft.
  2. Add salt, pepper and more water as needed.
  3. Add palm oil 5 to 10 minutes before it is done.
  4. Leave it longer if it has too much of water, depending on how you like it.
  5. Stir and it's ready to serve in 2 minutes.
